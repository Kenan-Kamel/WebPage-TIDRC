TIDRC Framwork

TIDRC (Framework of Touchscreen Interaction Design Recommendations for Children)
Framework is a scientific-based design methodology and practics to implment and desgin
interaction-based touchscreen applications for children. The framework focuses on the three
main aspects, cognitive, physical, and socio-emotional.

Social (S)

Provide choice and customization features to enhance children’s intrinsic motivation and task engagement

Consider using an open-ended app structure to support children’s engagement and creativity

Avoid computer-automated social interactions for children, as they have no effect on their engagement

*Provide an option for children to credit each other, rather than using automated means of acknowledgment
